Further instructions

Your save states and configuration will not be removed. To remove those files, delete the directory "%AppData%\Nooskewl\Monster RPG 2". %AppData% is usually 

"C:\Users\Your Username\AppData"

on Windows Vista and above, and 

"C:\Documents and Settings\Your Username\Application Data"

on Windows XP. Close this tab before deleting your save states and configuration, if you choose to do so.
